About me...

Hi there! I am Sudeep and this is my personal page. As you might have guessed from the title, I am a bioinformatician. This page is to tell a little bit about myself, what I is expertise/what I am working on and what are my interests and goals.

Most of my work has been on Next Generation Sequencing data: primarily on RNA-seq data. I have also been woking on ChIP-seq datasets, Ribo-seq datasets and a couple of custom "flavors" of sequencing data. In the past I have worked on CRISPR-Cas9 prediction pipeline and have been looking into integrative data analysis: combining data generated from biological experiments along with data and annotations from biological databases.

So is that it?

Well not quite... besides bioinformatics, I am also interested in data science and the whole flurry of fields associated with it. I have some experience in text mining and Named Entity recognition and I am interested in machine learning, data representation and visualization. I occassionaly post some of my work in GitHub and sometimes write up machine learning related kernels in Kaggle.
